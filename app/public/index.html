About

Who We Are?

We are a team of fundraisers based in Ukraine looking to raise money from around the world to improve healthcare in Ukraine

What We Do?

We spend our time fundraising for a cause we firmly believe in. Our mission is to raise money to invest in training and equipment for highly capable doctors in Ukraine

Why We Do It?

We see the state of healthcare in our country, and believe with enough fundraising we can drastically improve training for doctors for the benefit of all Ukrainians

Since When?

This began when we saw people needlessly passing away in our country because of a simple lack of doctor training and specialised equipment
